Introduction

Motionhaus

Showcasing motion design and animation trends, news, and inspiration, as well as highlighting work from popular designers such as Alexis Jamet and Studio Dunbar. Motionhaus magazine is a place for upcoming designers to stay up to date with the design world, learn tips and tricks, and hone their artistic eye all in one place.
